Silicon City

Pre-Feasibility Investment Framework · from ~USD 0.55 billion · Confidential

Silicon City is a phased advanced-technology manufacturing city built on the Al-Qaryatayn silica resource (~100 Mt indicated-equivalent, best assay 99.4% SiO₂ across 17 independent ALS Arabia analyses): from mining and beneficiation, through glass and silica-based building materials, to metallurgical-grade silicon, polysilicon in the electronic-grade class (industry standard 9N+, technology-partner dependent), and solar cell and module manufacturing. Each phase is gated on the one before — a sequence of independently bankable projects, not a single monolithic bet.

Phase A carries near-DD-grade financials, available below.

The binding constraint: firm electricity

The smelter needs ~45–55 MW continuous; polysilicon adds ~50–90 MW. The Syrian grid cannot supply this today. Phases A–B proceed on modest grid and captive power; Phases C–E are gated on a bankable power solution (hybrid solar + storage + thermal, grid rehabilitation, or a dedicated IPP). The first workstream for an anchor partner is a formal power options study.
